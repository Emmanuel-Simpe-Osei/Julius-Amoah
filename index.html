Meet Julius Amoah

Professional Celebrant | Certified Bookkeeper | Pastoral Leader

Julius Amoah is a professionally trained celebrant, certified by the Academy of Professional Celebrants, UK. He is qualified to officiate a range of life events including weddings, renewal of vows, funerals, and naming ceremonies. With a warm and compassionate presence, Julius creates meaningful and memorable ceremonies that truly reflect the lives and values of those involved.

In addition to his celebrant work, Julius is a certified bookkeeper and a proud member of the Institute of Certified Bookkeepers (UK). He is also a student affiliate of the Association of Chartered Certified Accountants (ACCA), UK, showcasing his ongoing commitment to excellence and professional growth.

Julius serves in a pastoral leadership role at New Springs Church, where his dedication to the emotional and spiritual wellbeing of others shines through. His empathetic approach and genuine care for people make him a trusted choice for families and individuals seeking guidance and support during both life's challenges and celebrations.

Whether you are planning a joyful wedding, a heartfelt memorial, or a special naming ceremony, Julius brings experience, sincerity, and a deep sense of purpose to every occasion.
