Julius Amoah is the founder of Connect Global Intercession Prayer Hub, International and currently the residing Pastor of New Spring Church, UK chapter.

He is a Revivalist and blessed with a strong Teaching grace. He ministers with passion and zest, and he is a firm believer of the death, burial and ascension of the Lord Jesus Christ.

He is also the Director of JKA Consultancy and Bookkeeping Ltd, an Accounting and Bookkeeping firm registered and located in the United Kingdom.

He is married to Lady Millicent Amoah, founder and leader of SHE SOARS, a thriving women's ministry. They are both blessed with two amazing children.
